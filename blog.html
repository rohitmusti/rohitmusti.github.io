Innovation and Product Development: IP and OSD solutions compared

I was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio. Growing up, I loved to play tennis and would spend hours and hours honing my abilities on the court. Off the court, my mom spent countless, thankless hours driving me to and from piano lessons, for which I am forever grateful.

I graduated from Indian Hill High School in 2016 and now study at the University of Virginia as a Jefferson Scholar. Perhaps the most beautiful town in the country, I have learned to love Charlottesville and now consider it my home. I am in my second year of study and I’m pursuing a 3 + 1 Bachelors in Computer Science and Masters in Computer Science Program. I have many academic interests and I have found that computer science is the best tool for weaving together my every interest, resulting in incredibly interesting projects and discovering connections I never imagined.
