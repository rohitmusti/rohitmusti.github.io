Why Computer Science

I fell in love with computer science because of how creative you can be with it; there is no one solution to any problem and there are constantly new skills and tools to be picking up. Similar to wood working, there are different methods of building out software, some more stable and robust while others are most delicate and refined. Computer Science has become a wonderful outlet for my creativity and constantly challenges me to learn new skills and stay up to date. It also rides the delicate balance between a purely engineering field and a purely creative field, with room to pursue both aspects.
