Our flowers are grown strategically

Most flower boutiques are do not grow their flowers with effective timing in mind. That is to say, their flowers will be fresh at the beginning of the season but that will be the batch they grow in massive bulk. In our fields, we grow our flowers in waves so that every month we have a fresh batch of flowers. We can also accommodate extremely large orders for large-scale with our green houses special flowers. We do carry some GMO flowers but do prefer to grow strains commonly found in nature.

Crossbreeding

Some folks like to focus on their efforts on breeding the 'perfect' flower.

Moreover, there are specialists on-site at our store that can work with your individual needs to crossbreed plants. They do this by controlling the pollination patterns of the flowers. There are also various soil and water mixtures we can use to promote different types of growth. By manipulating the amount and direction of the light, interesting shapes and flowers can be grown that would not otherwise be possible in nature. Keep in mind, this is all done without 'hard-wiring' the genetic code.
