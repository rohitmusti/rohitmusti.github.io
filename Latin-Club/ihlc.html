Why You Should Join

The classics influence everybody in their day to day lives and deserve to be celebrated. By joining the Indian Hill Latin Club, you are joining the Junior Classical League; an organization dedicated to remembering, preserving, and cellibrating the classics. At State and National Conventions, schools and states show off their dedication through incredible projects, competitive certanem (trivia), intense Ludi, and academic testing. When you join Indian Hill Latin Club, you are joining a nationwide group of people who are passionate about the classics. State and National conventions are a ton of fun and the Indian Hill chapter hosts exciting events every month, from Toga Tag to Convention Crams to keep the spirit alive throughout the year.
