What Exactly Do We Think is Required For Student Body Officers

I hope you consider all of these factors when voting

Experience

Student Body Officers are in charge of ALL of Student Government. It is VITAL that each member of a student body team have at least two years of experience on student government.

Organization

It is imperative that every single student body officer has the diligence and dedication to carry out their tasks. This of course involves being well organized.

Involvement

Each student body officer must be involved in a broad range of activities. Not only will this make them more accessible, but they can then understand what the different groups within school want and need.

Passion

Because of the responsibility and work that is involved with being a student body officer, it's important that they have a real passion for helping the school.
